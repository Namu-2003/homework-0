Advanced Features

Heatmap Analysis

Visualize attention patterns with customizable heatmaps. Our advanced algorithms process fixation data to create intuitive visual representations of user attention, showing where users focus most on your interface.

Scan Path Analysis

Track the sequence of eye movements with detailed scan path visualizations. Understand the natural flow of visual attention across your interface, revealing the order and timing of user gaze patterns.

Gaze Plot Analysis

Analyze individual gaze points and their distribution. Our gaze plot visualization helps you understand the exact points where users look, providing detailed insights into visual attention patterns.

Saccade Plot Analysis

Study rapid eye movements between fixation points. Our saccade plot visualization helps you understand how users move their eyes across the interface, revealing the dynamics of visual exploration.
